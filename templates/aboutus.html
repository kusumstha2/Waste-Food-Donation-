About Save2Serve

The Save2Serve Mission

Our mission is to connect excess food from donors to those in need, reducing food waste and hunger simultaneously. Using technology, we make sure that this surplus food quickly and easily reaches those who need it most.

At Save2Serve, we believe in a world where no food goes to waste and no person goes hungry. By partnering with individuals, businesses, and organizations, we make it simple for food donors to share their surplus with communities that can benefit from it. Our innovative platform uses smart algorithms and location-based technology to match donations with nearby charities, shelters, and food banks, maximizing efficiency and minimizing food spoilage.

Together, let’s fight food waste, feed those in need, and create a community built on compassion and responsibility. Join us in making a difference – one meal at a time.

Our Values

Open and honest

We want you to know how your donation is used and the impact you’re having around the world.

Every shared meal counts

Give what you can — whether it’s one meal or one year of meals — and know that it makes a difference.

We’re in it together

We want fighting hunger to be universal so that anyone, anywhere, can share the meal.
